Information information

This Laboratory course is offered annually as an elective course for First Year First Semester MTech (CS) students of Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata. Formally, the course is known as:

Data and File Structures Laboratory | aka PDS Lab
MTech (CS) First Year First Semester | Syllabus

Course Outline outline

The basic thrust of this Lab will be to learn programming languages and implementing data structures. The Lab will try to stick to the basic course outline as prescribed in the syllabus.
